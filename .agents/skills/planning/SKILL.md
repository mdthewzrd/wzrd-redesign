# Planning Skill

## Purpose
Core project planning and task management skill. Provides systematic approaches to breaking down work, estimating effort, and managing dependencies.

## Aliases & Redirects
This skill acts as a facade/aggregator for:
- `context-driven-development` - Context-aware planning
- `track-management` - Work unit management
- `workflow-patterns` - Development workflows
- `team-composition-patterns` - Team planning
- `parallel-feature-development` - Parallel work coordination
- `task-coordination-strategies` - Task coordination
- `multi-reviewer-patterns` - Review coordination

## When to Use
Use this skill for:
- Project planning and roadmapping
- Task breakdown and estimation
- Dependency analysis and management
- Resource allocation and scheduling
- Risk assessment and mitigation planning
- Team coordination and communication planning
- Progress tracking and reporting

## Planning Methodology
1. **Goal Definition** - Clear, measurable objectives
2. **Scope Definition** - In-scope vs out-of-scope
3. **Task Breakdown** - Hierarchical task decomposition
4. **Estimation** - Effort, time, resource estimation
5. **Dependency Mapping** - Task relationships and order
6. **Risk Assessment** - Identify and mitigate risks
7. **Schedule Creation** - Timeline with milestones
8. **Resource Allocation** - Assign people, tools, budget
9. **Progress Tracking** - Monitor and adjust plan

## Planning Artifacts
- **Product Requirements Document (PRD)** - What to build
- **Technical Specification** - How to build it
- **Task Breakdown Structure** - Detailed tasks
- **Dependency Graph** - Task relationships
- **Gantt Chart/Timeline** - Schedule visualization
- **Risk Register** - Identified risks and mitigations
- **Resource Allocation Matrix** - Who does what

## Success Metrics
- Plan covers all required work
- Estimates are accurate (within 20%)
- Dependencies are correctly identified
- Risks are appropriately mitigated
- Team understands and agrees with plan
- Progress can be tracked effectively
- Plan can be adjusted as needed
